About the Tool

The data presented below reflects Stage-Discharge relationships at the Riffle Cross-Sections (XSs) as modeled in HEC-RAS for each Point of Interest (POI). The “Stage”, or “Depth” here represents the MAX DEPTH of the XS, not the average.

The range of flows to be considered at each POI is dictated by the range of flows encompassed by the field survey data at that particular riffle. Other flow-limitations include HEC-RAS model stability at high flows, which were excluded from these curves accordingly.

Using The Tool

Choose a POI from the drop-down menu to view its stage-depth rating curve. Adjust the depth value in the box below either by typing in a value, or by using the up-down arrows. These arrows change the depth in increments of 0.1 ft, though it’s possible to type in a more precise depth to any desired number of decimal places.

Watch as the orange marker adjusts to the new depth, displaying the depth and corresponding flow at that depth alongside it.

There are also a number of graph tools which appear on the upper-right corner of the graph when you mouse over it, such as zoom. You can return to the original plot by hitting the home-shaped button.

Enjoy!
