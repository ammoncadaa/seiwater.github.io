Stockholm Environment Institute, Water Group

The Water Group at the Stockholm Environment Institute advances integrated approaches to freshwater assessment and policy, in five main areas: methods for integrated water analysis, capacity building, modeling climate change impacts on water resources, river basin assessments, and global water futures.
